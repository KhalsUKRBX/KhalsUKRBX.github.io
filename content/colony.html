Inspired by games like Dwarf Fortress and RimWorld, Colony Survival is my current passion project on Roblox.

The brief of the game is that you are in control of your colony that has crash-landed on an alien planet, and as such your goal is to keep your colonists alive from the elements, AI raids and events.

After doing many hours of research into RimWorld and its systems, the game's foundations and underlying systems have been constructed in such a way that player-generated content (yes! mods!) will be possible in future with just a couple lines of code in a sandboxed modulescript.
